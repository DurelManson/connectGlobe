Who We Are

Connect Globe is a trusted overseas job placement consultancy specializing in recruitment and workforce solutions across diverse industry segments. As a MEA-approved agency, we connect exceptional talent with verified employers throughout the Middle East and beyond.

We bridge the gap between industries, talent, and technology worldwide, delivering innovative solutions tailored to modern businesses. Our comprehensive services span international recruitment, custom software development, industrial equipment trade, event management, and investment consultancy.
