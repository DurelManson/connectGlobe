Our fee structure is transparent and varies based on the service level you choose:

For Job Seekers:

  • Registration Fee: Initial one-time fee for profile creation and verification
  • Service Fee: Charged only upon successful placement (job offer secured)
  • Documentation Support: Assistance with visa processing, attestation, and medical clearance (billed separately based on actual costs)

For Employers:

  • Consultation Fee: Initial assessment and requirement analysis
  • Recruitment Fee: Percentage of annual salary or flat fee per successful placement
  • Volume Discounts: Special rates for bulk hiring (10+ positions)

Key Benefits:

  • No success, no fee policy for job seekers (main service fee only charged after placement)
  • Flexible payment plans available
  • All government fees (visa, medical, attestation) are pass-through costs with no markup
  • Free initial consultation for employers

Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific requirements. We provide detailed cost breakdowns before you commit to any service.

Job seeker safety is our top priority. Our rigorous employer verification process includes:

  • Company Registration Verification: Validation of trade licenses, business permits, and legal status
  • MEA Compliance Check: Ensuring employer has necessary approvals for overseas recruitment
  • Background Research: Investigation of company reputation, financial stability, and track record
  • Site Visits: Physical verification of work locations and facilities (when possible)
  • Contract Review: Legal examination of employment contracts for fair terms and conditions
  • Past Placements Follow-up: Feedback from candidates previously placed with the employer
  • Compliance Audit: Verification of labor law compliance, working conditions, and salary payments

We maintain a verified employer database and only work with companies that meet our strict safety and ethical standards. Your security and career success are non-negotiable.
